# NOVA_VAULT: Institutional Analysis

Clinical audit of the NOVA_VAULT Micro-SaaS pipeline reveals structural patterns optimized for single-occupancy operator models.

- **Compute:** Edge-first deployment (Vercel/Cloudflare Workers) reducing node-dependency latency.
